Governance & Integrity

The standards that hold our advice accountable.

Our governance principles are not a posture — they are the operating model that makes our independence credible to clients, regulators and capital partners.

Our Pillars

Six commitments we hold ourselves to.

Independence

We do not represent vendors, contractors or interested parties. Our advice is structurally aligned with our clients alone.

Integrity

Zero tolerance for corruption, bribery and conflicts of interest. We decline engagements that compromise these.

Transparency

Methodologies, assumptions and limitations are disclosed upfront. We document why — not only what.

Accountability

We stand behind our recommendations. Where evidence shifts, we say so — and we correct course.

Sustainability

Environmental, social and governance considerations are integrated into every engagement — not bolted on.

Stakeholder Respect

From ministers to community leaders, we engage with seriousness, preparation and respect for time.

Operating Standards

How we work — codified.

Our engagement protocols are documented and audited internally. They are also available to clients on request.

  • Conflict-of-interest screening before every mandate
  • Independent quality assurance on all deliverables
  • Data protection & client confidentiality standards
  • Anti-bribery & anti-corruption policy
  • Whistle-blower protection & reporting channels
  • Annual ESG & governance review
“Our governance is the structural reason our independence is credible — not the marketing version of it.”
