Infrastructure Advisory Services

End-to-end advisory for infrastructure that endures.

From strategy and feasibility to transaction structuring, financial close and delivery oversight — Ask George supports clients across the full project lifecycle.

01 · Airports Advisory
SERVICE / 01

Airports & Aviation Advisory

Advisory across the airport lifecycle — from greenfield development to operational uplift, expansion and concession transactions.

We help governments and operators position aviation infrastructure for long-term commercial viability, with deep attention to traffic modelling, environmental considerations and PPP design.

  • Master-planning, capacity expansion & modernization roadmaps
  • Traffic forecasting, demand modelling & commercial assessments
  • Concession structuring, transaction advisory & investor engagement
  • Airport operations, management contracts & performance frameworks
  • Financing strategy and stakeholder coordination
02 · Toll Roads & Highways
SERVICE / 02

Toll Roads & Transport Infrastructure

Concession frameworks and bankable road investments — designed for credible procurement and durable operations.

We work alongside transport ministries, road authorities and investors to structure projects that reconcile traffic risk, affordability and long-term asset performance.

  • Feasibility, traffic & revenue studies
  • PPP structuring & concession framework design
  • Transaction advisory & procurement support
  • Tariff design, escalation regimes & regulatory frameworks
  • Implementation oversight & performance monitoring
03 · Ports & Logistics
SERVICE / 03

Ports & Logistics Advisory

Trade corridors, dry ports and maritime infrastructure — supporting the assets that move regional economies.

Strategic planning and operational uplift for ports and logistics hubs, with a sharp focus on commercial viability and corridor integration.

  • Strategic positioning & corridor competitiveness
  • Commercial assessments & market sizing
  • Operational advisory & concession design
  • Infrastructure development support & planning
  • Stakeholder coordination across customs, rail and shipping
04 · Hydro Power
SERVICE / 04

Hydro Power Projects

Technical, commercial and strategic advisory for hydro power and renewable energy investments.

We support project developers, public utilities and investors with bankable structures — from PPA negotiation to financial close.

  • Project structuring, due diligence & risk allocation
  • Power purchase agreement (PPA) advisory
  • Investment promotion & lender engagement
  • Regulatory & environmental compliance navigation
  • Implementation & commissioning support
05 · Electricity Transmission & Generation
SERVICE / 05

Electricity Transmission & Generation

Bankable power infrastructure — from utility-scale generation to cross-border transmission corridors.

Sector planning, regulatory advisory and transaction support for the projects that close energy access gaps and underpin industrialization.

  • Sector planning, generation expansion & integrated resource plans
  • Transmission & interconnection project advisory
  • Regulatory frameworks, tariff studies & market design
  • Investment promotion & transaction support
  • Independent power producer (IPP) structuring
06 · Government Advisory
SERVICE / 06

Government Advisory Services

Policy, institutional strengthening and governance advisory for public sector clients.

Whether reforming a sector, establishing a PPP unit or preparing for a flagship transaction — we partner with ministries, regulators and state-owned enterprises.

  • Policy & strategy development
  • Institutional strengthening & capacity building
  • Regulatory reviews & reform support
  • PPP framework design & rollout
  • Public sector studies & performance reviews
07 · Public-Private Partnerships
SERVICE / 07

Public-Private Partnerships (PPP)

Structuring and implementation support for PPP projects — the discipline of allocating risk to the party best able to manage it.

From feasibility through procurement and contract management, we work with public agencies and sponsors to design partnerships that hold up over decades.

  • PPP feasibility & value-for-money assessments
  • Risk analysis & allocation frameworks
  • Procurement strategy & tender support
  • Transaction advisory & contract structuring
  • Stakeholder coordination & negotiation support
08 · Governance & Institutional Studies
SERVICE / 08

Governance & Institutional Studies

Research, assessments and reviews for governments and development institutions navigating reform.

We produce evidence-based studies that inform policy, structure reform programs and strengthen the institutions responsible for delivering infrastructure outcomes.

  • Institutional assessments & diagnostic reviews
  • Governance frameworks & accountability structures
  • Sector studies & strategic reviews
  • Reform program design & implementation support
  • Research, evaluation & impact analysis
