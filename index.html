About me

A Telecommunications Engineer who turns ideas into dependable, well-designed digital products — where engineering meets a designer's eye and a consultant's grasp of the business.

I work at the intersection of software, data and IT infrastructure — architecting secure networks, managing servers, and building the websites, applications and data platforms that organisations rely on every day.

My journey runs from the network layer to the user interface: I've administered CISCO networks and cloud environments, run security audits and penetration tests, built custom content systems, and designed brands, reports and investment documents that help businesses look and operate at their best.

Today I lead IT and data systems at KK Advisory & Kwanza Infrastructure Group, while partnering with companies and entrepreneurs across the region on their digital products — always with the same promise: integrity, precision and hard work.
