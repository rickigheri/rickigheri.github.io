Who we are

An advisory practice rooted in the markets we serve.

Ask George Ltd. is an independent consultancy firm focused on infrastructure development, strategic advisory, and public sector transformation.

We work alongside governments, development agencies, investors, contractors, and private enterprises to create bankable, sustainable, and high-impact infrastructure solutions.

Our work spans transport, energy, governance and institutional advisory — but our orientation is consistent: long-term value, evidence-based recommendations and outcomes that endure.
